The concept of the entertaining and recreational complex “Lira”

Algarve Portugal
General layout and land improvement
Main Idea

The complex consists of several main and also a number of auxiliary facilities. The main constructions are a hotel, with the building area of 828.00 sq.m, a restaurant, the building area of 540.00 sq.m and a sports and recreational complex of 576 sq.m. There are three planned access points into the complex of the total area of 1.2 hectares. The main traffic stream is planned from the side of the hotel and the sports complex. All access points and approaches are stone paving. It is planned to build two eco-parking areas for 60 parking lots with a possibility to charge electric vehicles and also parking lots for bicycles. The project provides the proposals on land improvement within the area, vertical planning and gardening. In order to provide services to people with limited mobility (people with disabilities), the full range of actions is envisaged.


Hotel

The designed building is intended for accommodation of clients of a complex. The hotel is a five-storied building of 16 m high above the ground level and one technical floor below ground level, with the building area of 828.00 sq. m (category 4 ****, for 62 rooms) The building belongs to the main constructive system with longitudinal external and internal bearing walls. The building framework: monolithic reinforced concrete beams and columns The foundation: monolithic reinforced concrete raft External walls: ceramic block (380 mm thick) or brick of porous brick (380 mm thick). First floor plate: monolithic reinforced concrete (200 mm thick). Floor and roof slab: monolithic reinforced concrete (200 mm thick). Roof: heat-insulated membrane with an internal organized drain. Internal walls: ceramic block (250 mm thick) or brick block from a porous brick (250 mm thick) Partitions: gypsum board (with siding by plasterboard in two layers on both sides 150 mm thick with sound insulation from mineral wool slabs.

Improvement of the territory
Plan

Improvement of the territory provides for the construction of the blind area around the perimeter of all buildings. The walkways installation is going to be carried out from a natural, decorative stone and paving slabs. Also within the whole complex area there will be external energy-saving diode lighting. It is planned to place the modern street furniture and litter baskets along all walkways, also construction of the decorative lake, fountains and different small architectural pieces. For gardening will be developed a vegetation layout with the aim to select harmonious compositions from the variety of plants and trees in accordance with the climatic conditions. The territory of a complex around all perimeter is surrounded by a fence from decorative concrete elements of 1500 mm high and by a green hedge (bushes) Soft street lighting is provided for on solar batteries with lamps with photo cells: there is no need to have electric power for street lamps, therefore it is not required to run wires and to connect devices to the network. It will be significant saving of efforts and money. It is possible to install several dozens of lamps, but accounts for the electric power will not increase. Overall automation. Devices are charged, turn on and off without participation of the user. The installation of lamps will require minimum of time and efforts. As an option, devices can be easily rearranged to other places. Lamps are convenient in operation, not requiring complicated service. All this together creates the cozy atmosphere, esthetics and significant saving of energy resources.

Engineering networks
Water supply and sewerage

To provide a complex with water, it is planned to drill an artesian well or to connect to the local water supply networks. Also in order to receive a quality drinking water, there will be a station of advanced water treatment. The domestic sewage system will be connected to the existing local system. Small treatment facilities will be installed for purification of process (technical) waters. The swimming pools are considered as firewater tanks, that is why it is planned to install a small pumping station.

Power supply

Power supply of a complex is provided due to installation of roof solar batteries accumulating approx. 120 kW of electric power a day. Deficient volume of electric power will be compensated by connection to local electrical grid. All buildings will be equipped with the lightning protection and grounding, and also with surface safety systems for solar batteries against mechanical damages.

Heating, ventilation and conditioning

Heating will be done by installation of air recuperation and conditioning equipment. There will be compulsory supply and exhaust ventilation system with air recuperation. Conditioning - split air conditioning system.

Brazier zone

The building will be an arbour type for preparation of dishes on a brazier, a tandoor, a grill etc. The foundation is strip reinforced concrete foundation. Floor – natural stone. Walls – a natural stone, brick, metal frame. Roof – a metal roofing profile or metal tiles.
